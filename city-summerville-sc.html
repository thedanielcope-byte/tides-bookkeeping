Summerville Is SC's
Boomtown — and Business
Is Booming With It.

Summerville — "Flowertown in the Pines" — has transformed from a quiet Charleston suburb into one of the fastest-growing cities in the entire Southeast. Master-planned communities like Nexton and Carnes Crossroads have drawn tens of thousands of new residents, and the businesses that serve them are growing right alongside. The city now hosts a thriving downtown, major medical campuses, and a growing tech and professional services ecosystem.

🏥

Summerville Medical Center & Trident Health

Two major hospital campuses — Summerville Medical Center and Trident Medical Center — anchor a large and growing healthcare economy in the greater Summerville area.

🏗️

Master-Planned Community Boom

Nexton, Carnes Crossroads, Pine Hills, and Cane Bay Plantation have added thousands of homes and the retail, restaurant, and service businesses that follow new rooftops.

🌸

Downtown Flowertown Renaissance

Summerville's historic downtown has experienced a real revival — boutiques, restaurants, breweries, fitness studios, and professional services have transformed the Flowertown corridor into a genuine destination.
