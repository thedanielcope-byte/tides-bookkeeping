The Financial Headaches
We Fix

E-commerce and retail businesses face financial challenges that general bookkeepers routinely miss. Here's what we hear from nearly every new client.

😩

COGS Is Impossible to Track Manually

Knowing your true cost of goods sold across products, variants, and channels is the difference between a profitable product line and one that's quietly losing money.

📉

Platform Fees Eat Your Margins

Shopify, Amazon, PayPal, Stripe — every platform takes a cut, plus processing fees, plus monthly subscriptions. Without tracking it all, your margin analysis is useless.

🧾

Sales Tax Is a Compliance Nightmare

Selling in multiple states means navigating different sales tax rules in each one. We monitor your nexus exposure and make sure your records are ready for compliance.

😰

Multi-Channel Revenue Is a Reconciliation Mess

Shopify says one number. Amazon says another. Your bank account says something else. We consolidate every channel into one clean monthly picture.
