About the Area

Beaufort County: SC's
Coastal Crown Jewel
& Growth Leader.

Beaufort County is one of South Carolina's most dynamic and rapidly evolving markets. Anchored by the world-famous Hilton Head Island to the south and the historic City of Beaufort to the north, the county spans barrier islands, tidal marshes, and some of the fastest-growing inland corridors in the state. Bluffton — once a sleepy small town — has exploded into SC's fastest-growing municipality, drawing young families, remote workers, and retirees from across the East Coast.

The county's economy is uniquely layered: Hilton Head drives over 11 million annual visitors and a massive hospitality sector; MCAS Beaufort and Parris Island anchor a permanent military and defense-contractor economy; and the US-278 and US-17 corridors have become magnets for commercial development, retail, professional services, and light industry serving the region's booming residential population.

History & Economic Foundation

🏝️
Hilton Head Island — America's Premier Resort Town
From its development in the 1960s through today, Hilton Head has built one of the most resilient tourism economies in the Southeast — 11M+ annual visitors, 250+ restaurants, over 100 golf holes, and a hospitality workforce that sustains thousands of local businesses year-round.
🎖️
Parris Island & MCAS Beaufort — Military Backbone
Parris Island has trained every Marine who enlists on the East Coast for over a century. Combined with MCAS Beaufort (home of F/A-18 squadrons), the military presence generates thousands of permanent jobs and a large, stable consumer base for local businesses.
🏗️
Bluffton — The State's Fastest-Growing City
Bluffton's transformation from a small Lowcountry town into a booming suburb has been extraordinary. Master-planned communities, nationally branded retailers, a healthcare corridor along Buckwalter Pkwy, and a wave of small business formation have made it SC's most dynamic growth story.
🏥
Healthcare Anchors the Local Economy
Beaufort Memorial Hospital (the county's largest private employer), Hilton Head Regional Medical Center (HCA Healthcare), and a growing network of specialty clinics and urgent care facilities provide thousands of stable jobs and sustain a large professional services ecosystem.

Key Commercial Corridors & Routes

US-278 / William Hilton Pkwy
The main gateway to Hilton Head Island — commercial strip from the bridge to Sea Pines Circle, anchored by retail, restaurants, and resort-related services.
Bluffton Pkwy / Fording Island Rd
Bluffton's primary commercial spine — major retail centers, medical offices, restaurants, and the Buckwalter Pkwy healthcare corridor all branch from this route.
US-17 / Coastal Highway
The historic coastal corridor connecting Beaufort, Ridgeland, and the Georgia line — commercial development, hospitality, and growing logistics presence along the entire route.
SC-170 / Okatie Highway
The inland connector between Beaufort, Okatie, and the Bluffton growth zone — professional services, light industrial, and new residential support businesses line this fast-growing corridor.
Shelter Cove / Sea Pines (HHI)
Hilton Head's two premier commercial and resort districts — Shelter Cove Harbour for marina and waterfront dining, Sea Pines for luxury resort amenities and high-end retail.
I-95 Hardeeville Corridor
I-95 at the Georgia/SC line is booming — major distribution, logistics, and commercial development driven by Savannah port overflow and SC's competitive tax environment.
