Rock Hill Is Building
Its Own Identity — Fast.

Rock Hill is no longer just a Charlotte suburb. It's York County's anchor city — a mid-sized market with a revitalized downtown, a growing professional class, a world-class BMX and sports complex, and major employers across healthcare, education, and manufacturing. The city's Old Town district has become one of SC's most talked-about urban revival stories.

🏥

Piedmont Medical Center

3,500+ employees — Rock Hill's largest employer and the anchor of a growing healthcare ecosystem on the city's north side.

🎓

Winthrop University

6,000+ students and 1,400+ employees — a liberal arts and sciences university that drives a significant segment of Rock Hill's professional services, housing, and food and beverage economy.

🚲

Rock Hill Sports & Event Center

Home of the USA Cycling National Events Center — a catalyst for downtown revitalization, tourism, and new hospitality and retail development throughout the Old Town corridor.

Key Roads & Corridors

🛣️
I-77 — Charlotte to Columbia
Rock Hill's economic spine — connecting the city to Charlotte (30 min north) and Columbia (60 min south). The primary corridor for commuters, logistics, and businesses in the Charlotte MSA.

US-21 / Cherry Road
Rock Hill's main commercial strip — dense retail, medical, and service businesses from downtown north to the Fort Mill line.
