Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Can I change plans later? +
Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a setup fee? +
No setup fees, ever. You pay the flat monthly rate and nothing else. Onboarding and integration are included.
What if my expenses go over the plan limit? +
We'll reach out before that happens and help you move to the right plan. No surprise charges — we always communicate first.
What software do you use? +
We primarily use QuickBooks Online and Puzzle.io, but we integrate with all major platforms. Your software subscription cost is included in your plan.
Do you do catch-up bookkeeping? +
Yes. If you have a backlog of unreconciled books, we handle the catch-up before starting your monthly service. Catch-up pricing is quoted separately based on how far behind you are.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time with 30 days notice — no penalties, no hassle.
Do you work with my CPA? +
Yes — we work alongside your existing CPA or tax preparer and keep your books organized in exactly the format they need for filing. We also offer CPA consultations directly through Tides.
How quickly can I get started? +
Fast. After your free consultation, most clients are fully onboarded and running in 2–3 business days. We handle the setup so you don't have to lift a finger.
