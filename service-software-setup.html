What We Build or
Fix for You.

A correct accounting setup is the foundation everything else depends on. Here's what we configure — whether we're building from scratch or cleaning up an existing mess.

✅

Chart of Accounts Setup

We build or restructure your chart of accounts to match your industry, your business model, and IRS expense categories. Every account in the right place — no more dumping everything in "Other Expenses."

✅

Bank & Credit Card Feed Configuration

All business bank accounts and credit cards are connected to QuickBooks via live bank feeds. Transactions flow in automatically, categorized correctly from day one.

✅

Opening Balance Verification

Starting balances are verified against your actual bank statements and prior records. If they're off, we find out why and fix it — so your books are accurate from the first entry.

✅

Duplicate & Error Cleanup

Duplicate transactions, manual entries that conflict with bank feeds, and misclassified items are identified and removed. Your transaction history is clean.

Wrong Setup Costs
More Than You Think.

An incorrect QuickBooks setup doesn't just produce wrong reports — it corrupts your financial history and makes every decision based on that data wrong too.

📊

Reports You Can't Trust

If your chart of accounts is wrong, your P&L is wrong. Revenue in the wrong account, expenses miscategorized — the numbers look fine until you compare them to reality.

💸

CPA Cleanup Bills

Every error your accountant has to fix at tax time adds to their bill. A QBO setup done wrong from the start typically costs $500–$2,000 extra at year-end.

🔁

Duplicate Transactions

Bank feeds plus manual entry plus imported data = duplicate transactions everywhere. They inflate both revenue and expenses in ways that are hard to spot.

🏛️

Audit Risk

Miscategorized expenses and unreconciled accounts are red flags for auditors. A clean, accurate setup dramatically reduces your risk profile.
