Our Story

Built on the Belief That
Every Business Deserves
Clean Books.

Tides Bookkeeping was founded in Greenville, South Carolina with one goal in mind: give small business owners the financial clarity they need to grow with confidence. Too many great businesses struggle not because they lack drive, but because they don't have an accurate, up-to-date picture of their finances.

We set out to change that. We built a remote bookkeeping service that combines the attentiveness of a local firm with the efficiency of a modern, tech-forward operation. One flat monthly rate. One dedicated bookkeeper. Clean books every single month.

Today, we serve clients across industries — from real estate investors and contractors to travel agencies, healthcare practices, and tech startups. No matter the business, our approach is the same: accurate books, proactive communication, and reports you can understand without an accounting degree.

What We Stand For

The Values Behind
Every Decision We Make

We exist to give business owners clarity and confidence in their finances — so they can focus on running their business, not chasing receipts. These core values shape how we work every day.

⚡

Accuracy Over Speed

Get it right the first time. Errors in bookkeeping cost clients time and money — precision is non-negotiable in everything we do.

📣

Proactive Communication

No surprises. We flag issues early, update clients before they have to ask, and keep everyone in the loop — always.

✨

Simplicity

Make finances easy to understand. If a client can't follow it, we haven't done our job well enough. Clarity is the goal.

🤝

Ownership

We treat every client's books like our own business. Full responsibility — no blame-shifting, no excuses, just results.

⚙️

Systematize Everything

If we do it twice, we automate it. We build systems that scale so our team spends time on high-value work — not repetitive tasks — and our clients get consistent, reliable results month after month.
